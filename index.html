My Work

ProCon Redesign Case Study

ProCon.org is a nonprofit online encyclopedia that offers pro and con arguments on debatable issues. Their motto; “Understand The Issues. Understand Each Other.”. When using ProCon.org users feel confused and frustrated when looking for topics and issues due to the layout and navigation. In addition, first-time users are not quickly introduced to who ProCon.org is and why they provide their services causing a delay in building valuable trust with the site.

Tern Mobile App Case Study

Users may typically plan a trip to a particular destination, but they may not always have plans to fill the gaps, especially on longer trips. Tern is a travel companion app that serves as a means to make both local food and activity recommendations to you based on where you’re located at.
